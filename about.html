About Memora

Our mission, vision, and the story behind our innovative platform

Our Story

Memora was born from a deeply personal experience. Our founder, Dr. Elena Chen, witnessed her grandmother's struggle with dementia and the profound impact it had on her family. Despite being surrounded by love and support, her grandmother faced daily challenges with memory loss and maintaining independence.

After extensive research and collaboration with healthcare professionals, Dr. Chen assembled a team of experts in neuroscience, technology, and caregiving to create a comprehensive solution that addresses the multifaceted needs of individuals with dementia.

Since our founding in 2023, Memora has grown from a small startup to a leading innovator in dementia care technology, helping thousands of families navigate the challenges of dementia with dignity and compassion.

Our Mission

To enhance the quality of life for individuals with dementia by preserving memories, supporting emotional well-being, and promoting independence through innovative technology.

Our Vision

A world where individuals with dementia can live with dignity, purpose, and joy, supported by technology that adapts to their unique needs and preserves their personal legacy.

Our Core Values

Compassion

We approach every aspect of our work with empathy and understanding for the unique challenges faced by individuals with dementia and their caregivers.

Innovation

We continuously push the boundaries of technology to create solutions that meaningfully address the complex needs of dementia care.

Inclusivity

We design our platform to be accessible and beneficial for people of all backgrounds, abilities, and stages of dementia.

Integrity

We uphold the highest standards of privacy, security, and ethical practice in all our operations and interactions.

Empowerment

We strive to give individuals with dementia greater control over their lives and provide caregivers with tools that reduce burden and enhance care.

Evidence-Based

We ground our approach in scientific research and collaborate with experts to ensure our platform delivers meaningful outcomes.
