Frequently Asked Questions

How does Memora help individuals with dementia?

Memora provides comprehensive support through memory preservation features, routine management tools, emotional support, and safety monitoring. Our platform adapts to changing cognitive abilities and offers personalized experiences based on individual preferences and needs.

Is Memora suitable for all stages of dementia?

Yes, Memora is designed to support individuals across all stages of dementia. Our adaptive interface and features adjust to changing cognitive abilities, providing appropriate support whether someone is in the early, middle, or advanced stages of dementia.

How secure is the data stored in Memora?

We take data security and privacy extremely seriously. Memora uses end-to-end encryption, secure cloud storage, and follows all relevant healthcare data protection regulations. We never share personal data with third parties without explicit consent.

Can multiple family members access and contribute to a Memora account?

Yes, Memora supports a "Care Circle" feature that allows multiple family members and caregivers to access and contribute to an account. Different permission levels can be set to control who can view, edit, or manage various aspects of the platform.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes, we offer a 30-day free trial that gives you full access to all Memora features. No credit card is required to start the trial, and you'll receive a reminder before the trial period ends.

What devices is Memora compatible with?

Memora is available as a web application and as native apps for iOS and Android devices. This ensures you can access the platform from smartphones, tablets, and computers, providing flexibility for both users and caregivers.
