Our Features

Discover how Memora supports individuals with dementia and their caregivers

Comprehensive Support System

Memora offers a holistic approach to dementia care, combining cutting-edge technology with compassionate design to address the complex needs of individuals with dementia and their caregivers. Our platform is built on three core pillars:

Memory Preservation

Capturing and revisiting meaningful memories to strengthen cognitive connections and provide emotional comfort.

Emotional Support

Providing personalized interactions and resources to enhance mood, reduce anxiety, and foster connection.

Routine Management

Facilitating daily activities, medication adherence, and safety monitoring to promote independence and reduce caregiver burden.

Memory Preservation

Preserving What Matters Most

Our memory preservation features help individuals with dementia maintain connections to their personal history and identity, while providing moments of joy and recognition.

Digital Memory Album

Create and organize collections of photos, videos, and audio recordings that capture important life moments, relationships, and achievements.

Daily Memory Flashbacks

Receive daily reminders of meaningful memories through our intelligent algorithm that selects appropriate content based on mood and time of day.

Voice Memoirs

Record and preserve personal stories and reflections with our easy-to-use voice recording feature, creating an audio legacy for future generations.

Family Contribution

Allow family members to remotely add photos, videos, and messages to the memory album, fostering connection and shared reminiscence.

Smart Reminders & Routine Management

Supporting Daily Independence

Our routine management system helps maintain structure and predictability, essential elements for individuals with dementia to navigate daily life with greater confidence and autonomy.

Medication Management

Receive timely medication reminders with visual cues and confirmation tracking to ensure proper adherence to treatment plans.

Meal & Hydration Reminders

Get gentle prompts for regular meals and hydration, customized to personal preferences and dietary requirements.

Personal Care Assistance

Step-by-step guidance for personal care routines with adaptive instructions based on cognitive ability and time of day.

Appointment Tracker

Manage healthcare appointments with reminders, transportation arrangements, and preparation instructions.

AI-Powered Support

Intelligent Assistance

Our artificial intelligence features adapt to individual needs and preferences, providing personalized support that evolves with changing cognitive abilities.

Cognitive Exercises

Engage with personalized brain games and activities designed to stimulate cognitive function and adapt to changing abilities.

Activity Suggestions

Receive tailored recommendations for meaningful activities based on personal history, preferences, and current cognitive state.

Conversational Companion

Interact with our AI companion for stimulating conversations, reminiscence support, and emotional connection.

Pattern Recognition

Our AI analyzes behavior patterns to identify potential issues early and suggest preventive interventions.

Safety & Caregiver Support

Peace of Mind for Everyone

Our safety features and caregiver tools create a secure environment for individuals with dementia while providing essential support and insights for caregivers.

Location Tracking

Optional GPS monitoring with safe zone alerts to prevent wandering and ensure quick response if needed.

Emergency Alerts

One-touch emergency button that immediately notifies designated caregivers with location information.

Caregiver Dashboard

Comprehensive overview of daily activities, medication adherence, mood patterns, and cognitive engagement.

Care Circle Coordination

Coordinate care responsibilities among multiple caregivers with task assignments and communication tools.
