The Challenge

Millions of people with dementia struggle with memory, cognition, and daily life, leading to greater dependence on caregivers. Current solutions are limited and often fail to address the comprehensive needs of individuals with dementia.

  • Progressive memory loss
  • Difficulty maintaining daily routines
  • Emotional distress and confusion
  • Increased caregiver burden
  • Limited cognitive stimulation
